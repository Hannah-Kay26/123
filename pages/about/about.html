About Us

Hi there!

We are Dori, Jessica, and Hannah, and we want to change the way you start your mornings.

We believe a balanced and nutritious breakfast is paramount to starting the day off right, and understand the need for a little pick-me-up to help people shake off that morning slump— but instead of chugging a cup of coffee for that energy boost, why not get it from a delicious cereal?

How Buzz Bites Began

Our story began in 2024, when we’d finally had enough of our breakfast-less days. Every day, it would be the same: wake up to the morning alarm, shower, brush our teeth, get dressed, and get out the door in thirty minutes, all so we can start our days on time. In the process, the most important meal of the day would almost always fall to the wayside: breakfast.

In lieu of a nutritious and filling breakfast, we had to settle for a quick cup of coffee, hurriedly consumed right before we left or during our commute. While the energy was much needed, we wondered if we really had to sacrifice adequate nutrition for a caffeine boost. What if we could get our pick-me-up and our breakfast in at the same time?

And so, Buzz Bites was born. A delightful blend of honey-toasted oats, multigrain flakes, and clusters of roasted nuts and seeds, Buzz Bites promises a satisfying crunch in every spoonful. The cereal is lightly sweetened with natural sugars and enhanced with a hint of vanilla for a delicious morning treat— and of course, contains the equivalent caffeine of a small cup of coffee in every serving, providing a gentle and sustained energy lift. Say goodbye to skipping breakfast, and say hello to a more fulfilling morning.
