Ingredients

Our Key Ingredients





Green Tea Extract

This is our main ingredient as this is where our source of caffeine from our cereal comes from. It contains 50mg of caffeine. Green tea extract comes from the leaves of the Camellia sinensis plant and is highly concentrated. It contains potent antioxidants–such as flavonoids and polyphenols like epigallocatechin gallate (EGCG)–that protect against cell damage and offer a variety of health benefits. Some of its health benefits are improved alertness, mood, healthier skin, weight loss etc. Our green tea extract is made with all healthy ingredients which are meant to improve your health.



USDA-Approved Honey

Honey is a natural product produced by bees. Honey is not only used as a nutritional product but also as medicine which is why we decided to make it one of our main ingredients. Our honey is 100% raw and unfiltered which means it preserves all the natural nutrients, enzymes, and antioxidants that make it beneficial for your health. We avoid any processing methods that could compromise the quality of our honey like heating. Our beekeepers are committed to providing you with the purest and most delicious honey that we can offer.



Non-GMO Oats

Our oats are sourced from an organically certified farm that grows non-genetically modified crops without the use of pesticides. Our farmers are dedicated to producing healthy food. Oats have several components that are good for your health. Oats have a type of soluble fiber called beta-glucan which is known to slow digestion, suppress appetite and increase satiety. We also have strict systems in place for testing for GMO in our products. To reduce GMO risk, our products are processed in Non-GMO Project Verified facilities.
