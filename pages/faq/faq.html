Frequently Asked Questions

We've got answers to your buzzes!

Where can I find your products?

You can check out our products by going to our navigation and clicking on Products at the top of the page. You can also find our products in stores near you by going to the Retail Locations page, which is under Buy Now.

Where is the expiration date on your products?

You can find the expiration date on the back of the box.

How many flavors does Buzz Bites have?

Buzz Bites currently comes in three flavors, which are: Honey Buzz, Coco Buzz and Apple Buzz.

What ingredients are in Buzz Bites?

Our three main ingredients are Green Tea Extract, Non-GMO Oats and Grade-A USDA-Approved Honey. You can find more information in our Ingredients page, and additional ingredients for each flavor on our Products page.

Can your boxes be recycled?

Our boxes are made of cardboard and are recyclable.

What does Buzz Bites taste like?

Buzz Bites has a unique taste to each individual and the taste gets better with every bite. You can check out our different flavors on our Products page!

How much caffeine is in a serving of Buzz Bites? Is it safe?

Our cereal contains 50mg of caffeine per serving. It is very safe as it is well below the maximum intake of caffeine per day, which is 400mg.

Are there going to be more flavors?

Although we offer three flavors right now, we are hard at work formulating more delicious flavors for the future! Contact us via phone or email and let us know what flavors you would like to see.

How long is the shelf life of Buzz Bites?

The shelf life of an unopened box of Buzz Bites is 12 months.

Are there any allergens in your cereal?

Wheat is the main allergen in our cereal. You can look at the ingredients of each product on our Products page to make sure it doesn't have an ingredient you are allergic to.
